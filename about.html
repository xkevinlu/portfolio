Kevin Lu is designing the future of work at ADP

Hi, I’m Kevin. I believe that great products are a means to an end, and should help us actualize our own goals, desires, and best self.

I love to prototype and make things. I'm currently working on a design education game called Pixel Perfect.

In the past, I redesigned the sign-up flow for fuboTV, a sports-streaming startup, and have worked as a freelance front-end developer designing and building websites for small business owners.

My background is in Behavioral Economics. I was a research assistant at the Aresty Research Center studying cognitive biases and decision making. I earned a B.S. in Economics and certificate of Quantitative Economics from Rutgers University.

I’m currently based in Manhattan, NY.
